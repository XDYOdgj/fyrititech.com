Genina Waterproof Chair Seat Covers

Enhance your seating experience with these Genina waterproof chair seat covers. Designed with a durable waterproof coating and a specialized PE film inner layer, they provide double protection against spills, stains, and pet accidents. Whether you're hosting a dinner party or enjoying a quiet family meal, these covers keep your furniture clean and safe without compromising style.

Featuring a stretchable jacquard fabric, these covers are easy to slide on and secure with an elastic band and buckle. This ensures a snug fit on most standard chairs, making them a practical and elegant solution for everyday use. The unique flower pattern in khaki adds a pop of color to your dining room, kitchen, or hotel space, blending functionality with aesthetics.

Perfect for families, pet owners, and anyone looking for reliable chair protection, these covers are versatile and easy to maintain. Choose from a range of applications, such as dining rooms, kitchens, hotels, and more. Simply hand wash or machine wash with cold water and enjoy long-lasting durability and a fresh, decorative appearance.
