Levoberg Dark Gray Stool Covers

Upgrade your furniture with the sleek and functional Levoberg Dark Gray Stool Covers. These sleek, modern covers are crafted from a premium blend of 95% polyester and 5% spandex jacquard fabric, offering a soft yet durable touch that adds comfort without compromising strength. Whether tucked under the counter or replacing worn bar stools, they deliver a fresh, custom look that easily transforms your space.

These versatile stool covers are perfect for a variety of settings including hotels, cafes, bars, and your home. Designed to fit most stool sizes, they provide an elegant solution for protecting your wooden or metal chairs from daily wear, spills, scratches, and stains. With an elastic band and belt system, they ensure a secure, snug fit that stays in place without slipping, making them ideal for both casual and professional environments.

The stretch jacquard fabric offers a luxurious feel while resisting wrinkles and shrinkage for long-lasting performance. Plus, their machine washable design makes cleaning and maintenance effortless, allowing you to keep your furniture looking brand new with minimal effort.
