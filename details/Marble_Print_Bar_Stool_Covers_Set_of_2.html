Stylish and Protective Bar Stool Covers

These Marble Print Bar Stool Covers are designed to enhance both the look and functionality of your rectangular bar stools. Made from a premium blend of 90% polyester and 10% spandex, they offer a flexible yet durable fit for stools up to 20 inches in length and 14 inches in width. The soft, stretchy material ensures a snug and comfortable wrap, while also protecting against spills, stains, and scratches.

Perfect for families with kids or pets, these covers are a practical way to keep your seating area clean and stylish. Their elastic band design makes installation easy and secure, providing a hassle-free solution for both home and commercial spaces. The decorative marble print adds a touch of elegance, making them ideal for use in kitchens, dining rooms, patios, bars, and restaurants.

Each set includes two covers, offering the convenience of styling multiple stools at once. This means you can keep your space looking fresh and inviting without the hassle of cleaning or replacing your existing stools. Whether you're looking to upgrade your decor or protect your investment, these covers deliver both form and function with ease.
